Vista Computer Repair Project Analysis

By Chris Vigneault

Site Intent:

The main intent of this redesign is to organize and condense the site into about six pages of focused content. Running a validation check on www.vistacomputerrepair.com at validator.w3.org comes up with 424 errors and 37 warnings, this alone is reason enough to redesign the site. Further checking shows validation errors on all pages of the site. Other concerns include fixing page navigation to be global navigation across entire site and utilizing semantically correct and w3c validated code.
